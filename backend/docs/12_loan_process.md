## Applying for a Personal or Auto Loan
ZX Bank offers competitive rates and flexible terms for all your lending needs. Our application process is designed to be quick and efficient.

## Steps to Apply
1. **Pre-qualification**: Check your potential rates without affecting your credit score on our website.
2. **Submit Application**: Complete the full application form online or in a branch.
3. **Documentation**: Provide proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns) and employment verification.
4. **Review**: Our underwriters will review your credit history, income, and debt-to-income ratio.
5. **Approval and Funding**: If approved, sign the loan agreement electronically. Funds are often deposited into your ZX account within 24 hours.

## Common Requirements
- Minimum credit score of 620.
- Stable employment history for at least 2 years.
- Debt-to-income ratio below 40%.

## Repayment Options
We offer various repayment methods, including automatic monthly payments which may qualify you for an interest rate discount of 0.25%.
