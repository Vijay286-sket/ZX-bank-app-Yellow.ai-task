## Understanding KYC
Know Your Customer (KYC) is a mandatory process for all financial institutions to verify the identity of their clients. This helps us prevent fraud, money laundering, and other illegal activities.

## Our Verification Process
When you open an account or request specific services, we perform a series of checks to confirm your identity.

### Identity Checks
We verify your government-issued ID against official records. In some cases, we may use facial recognition technology to match your selfie with your ID photo.

### Address Verification
We confirm your residential address through third-party databases or by reviewing your provided proof of address documents.

### Background Screening
We check public records and sanction lists to ensure compliance with international financial regulations.

## Why It Matters
KYC protects both the bank and our customers. By ensuring we know who our customers are, we can provide a safer banking environment for everyone.

## Updates to Information
You are required to notify ZX Bank of any significant changes to your personal information, such as a change of address or legal name, to keep your records up to date and compliant.
