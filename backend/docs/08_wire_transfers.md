## Sending a Wire Transfer
Wire transfers are a fast and secure way to send large sums of money. ZX Bank supports both domestic and international wire transfers.

## Domestic Wire Transfers
To send a domestic wire, you will need:
- Recipient's Full Name and Address
- Recipient's Bank Name and Address
- Recipient's Routing Number (9 digits)
- Recipient's Account Number

**Fees**: $25 per outgoing domestic wire.

## Deadlines and Processing
- **Same-Day Processing**: Wires submitted before 4:00 PM EST on business days are processed the same day.
- **Arrival Time**: Funds typically arrive within 24 hours for domestic transfers.

## Security and Verification
For your protection, wire transfers above $10,000 may require a verbal callback verification from our security team. Ensure your phone number on file is up to date.
