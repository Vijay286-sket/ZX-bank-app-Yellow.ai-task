## Visit a ZX Bank Branch
Our branches are designed to provide you with personalized financial advice and specialized services.

## Branch Hours
Standard operating hours for most ZX Bank branches are:
- **Monday - Friday**: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
- **Saturday**: 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM
- **Sunday**: Closed

*Note: Hours may vary by location. Please use the 'Find a Branch' tool on our website for specific details.*

## Services Available
- **Personal Banking Consulting**: Meet with an advisor for account planning.
- **Loan Applications**: Apply for personal, auto, or mortgage loans in person.
- **Safe Deposit Boxes**: Securely store your valuables (available at select locations).
- **Notary Services**: complimentary notary services for ZX Bank customers.

## Scheduling an Appointment
To avoid wait times, we recommend scheduling an appointment through our mobile app or website before your visit.
