## Earn While You Spend
The ZX Credit Card Rewards Program is designed to give back to our loyal customers. Every purchase brings you closer to exciting rewards.

## Earning Points
- **Groceries & Dining**: Earn 3x points for every $1 spent.
- **Travel & Gas**: Earn 2x points for every $1 spent.
- **Other Purchases**: Earn 1x point for every $1 spent.

## Redemption Options
### Cash Back
Every 1,000 points can be redeemed for $10 statement credit or direct deposit into your ZX savings account.

### Travel
Use your points to book flights, hotels, and car rentals through our ZX Travel Portal. Points have 25% more value when redeemed for travel.

### Gift Cards
Redeem points for gift cards from hundreds of popular retailers and restaurants.

## Expiration
Points do not expire as long as your account remains open and in good standing. There is no cap on the number of points you can earn.
