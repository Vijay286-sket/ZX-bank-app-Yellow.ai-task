## Secure Your Future with Fixed Deposits
Fixed Deposits (FD), also known as Certificates of Deposit (CD), offer a guaranteed rate of return for a set period.

## Available Terms
We offer FD terms ranging from 6 months to 5 years:
- **6 Months**: 4.75% APY
- **12 Months**: 5.10% APY
- **24 Months**: 4.85% APY
- **36 Months**: 4.60% APY
- **60 Months**: 4.50% APY

## Key Terms and Conditions
### Minimum Deposit
The minimum opening deposit for a ZX Fixed Deposit is $1,000.

### Early Withdrawal Penalty
Withdrawing funds before the maturity date will result in a penalty:
- **Terms < 1 year**: 90 days of simple interest.
- **Terms >= 1 year**: 180 days of simple interest.

### Maturity Options
Upon maturity, you can:
1. Reinvest the principal and interest for the same term at current rates.
2. Withdraw the full amount (principal plus interest).
3. Transfer the funds to your ZX savings or checking account.

*If no action is taken within 10 days of maturity, the FD will automatically renew for the same term.*
