## How to Deposit a Check
Depositing a check is now easier than ever with the ZX Bank Mobile App. No need to visit a branch or ATM.

## Steps for Deposit
1. **Endorse the Check**: Sign the back of your check and write "For Mobile Deposit at ZX Bank Only" below your signature.
2. **Open the App**: Log in to your ZX Bank Mobile App.
3. **Select Deposit**: Tap on the 'Deposit' icon at the bottom of the screen.
4. **Enter Amount**: Type in the exact amount shown on the check.
5. **Take Photos**: Capture clear images of both the front and back of the endorsed check against a dark, flat surface.
6. **Confirm**: Review the details and submit the deposit.

## Important Guidelines
- **Limits**: Daily and monthly limits apply based on your account type. Individual checks cannot exceed $5,000.
- **Wait Time**: Standard hold periods apply. Funds are typically available within 1-2 business days.
- **Keep the Check**: Retain the physical check for 10 days after the deposit is confirmed before securely destroying it.
