## Start Your Journey with ZX Bank
Opening an account with ZX Bank is a straightforward process that can be completed online or in person at any of our branches.

## Eligibility
- Must be at least 18 years old (Minors can open custodial accounts).
- Must have a valid Social Security Number (SSN) or Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN).
- Must be a legal resident of a country where ZX Bank operates.

## Required Documents
- **Primary Identification**: A valid government-issued photo ID (e.g., Driver's License, Passport, State ID).
- **Secondary Identification**: One additional form of ID (e.g., Social Security card, birth certificate, credit card from another bank).
- **Proof of Address**: A utility bill, lease agreement, or bank statement dated within the last 60 days.

## Initial Deposit
A minimum initial deposit is required to open most accounts.
- **Basic Checking**: $25
- **Standard Savings**: $50
- **Premium Accounts**: $1,000

## Online Application
If applying online, you will need to scan and upload copies of the required documents and complete an electronic signature process.
