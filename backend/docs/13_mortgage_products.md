## Home Financing Options
ZX Bank offers a variety of mortgage products to help you achieve the dream of homeownership.

## Our Mortgage Types
### Fixed-Rate Mortgages
Your interest rate and monthly principal and interest payments remain the same for the life of the loan. Available in 15, 20, and 30-year terms.

### Adjustable-Rate Mortgages (ARM)
Typically start with a lower interest rate for an initial period (e.g., 5, 7, or 10 years), after which the rate adjusts periodically based on market conditions.

### First-Time Homebuyer Programs
Special programs with lower down payment requirements (as low as 3%) and flexible credit guidelines for those buying their first home.

## The Mortgage Process
1. **Pre-approval**: Get a clear idea of how much you can borrow.
2. **House Hunting**: Find your dream home with confidence.
3. **Formal Application**: Provide detailed financial information and property details.
4. **Appraisal and Inspection**: Ensure the property value and condition are acceptable.
5. **Closing**: Sign the final documents and get the keys to your new home.

## Expert Guidance
Our mortgage specialists are available to guide you through every step of the process and help you choose the best product for your financial situation.
