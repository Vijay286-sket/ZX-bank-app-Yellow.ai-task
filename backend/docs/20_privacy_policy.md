## Your Privacy is Important
At ZX Bank, we are committed to protecting the personal and financial information of our customers. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

## Information We Collect
- **Personal Information**: Name, address, SSN, date of birth.
- **Financial Information**: Transaction history, account balances, credit scores.
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with our website and mobile app.

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide and manage your banking services.
- To process transactions and send related communications.
- To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
- To prevent fraud and enhance security.
- To personalize your experience and improve our offerings.

## Sharing Your Information
We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:
- Service providers who help us operate our business.
- Regulatory authorities as required by law.
- Credit bureaus for credit reporting purposes.

## Your Choices
You have certain rights regarding your data, including:
- Opting out of marketing communications.
- Reviewing and updating your personal information.
- Accessing your data and requesting corrections.

## Data Security
We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.
