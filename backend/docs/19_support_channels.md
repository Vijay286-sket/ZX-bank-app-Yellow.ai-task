## We Are Here to Help
ZX Bank offers multiple ways to get in touch with our customer support team. Choose the method that is most convenient for you.

## Support Options
### 24/7 Phone Support
Call us anytime at 1-800-ZX-BANK-0 for general inquiries, account assistance, or technical support.

### Secure Messaging
Log in to the ZX Bank Mobile App or Online Banking and use our Secure Message Center to send a message to a representative. We typically respond within 1-2 hours.

### Live Chat
Available on our website and in the app from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM EST, Monday through Sunday. Look for the chat icon in the bottom right corner.

### Branch Network
Visit any ZX Bank branch during operating hours for in-person assistance.

### Social Media
Follow us on Twitter and Facebook @ZXBankSupport for quick updates and general questions. For security reasons, never share account details over social media.

## Escalations
If your issue is not resolved to your satisfaction, you can request to speak with a supervisor or file a formal complaint through our Quality Assurance department.
