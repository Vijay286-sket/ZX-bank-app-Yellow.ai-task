## Sending Money Overseas
ZX Bank provides reliable international wire transfer services to over 200 countries. We utilize the SWIFT network to ensuring your funds reach their destination safely.

## Information Required for International Wires
To send an international transfer, you will need the following details from the recipient:
- Recipient's Full Legal Name
- Recipient's Full Physical Address
- Recipient's Bank Name and Branch Address
- **SWIFT/BIC Code**: The unique identifier for the recipient's bank.
- **IBAN/Account Number**: The recipient's bank account number, often in IBAN format for Europe and the Middle East.

## Fees and Exchange Rates
- **Outgoing Fee**: $45 per international wire.
- **Exchange Rates**: Real-time exchange rates apply at the time of the transaction. A small conversion margin may be included.

## Delivery Times
International wires typically take between 2 to 5 business days to reach the recipient, depending on the destination country and any intermediary banks involved.

## Tracking
Once initiated, you will receive a reference number that can be used to track the status of your transfer through our international support desk.
