## About ZX Bank
ZX Bank is a leading global financial institution committed to providing innovative and secure banking solutions. Founded in 1995, we have grown to serve over 10 million customers worldwide.

## Our Mission
To empower individuals and businesses by providing accessible, transparent, and efficient financial services that drive growth and prosperity.

## Core Values
- **Integrity**: We uphold the highest ethical standards.
- **Innovation**: We constantly evolve to meet modern banking needs.
- **Customer Centricity**: Your financial well-being is our priority.
- **Security**: Protecting your assets and data is at the heart of everything we do.

## Key Offerings
We provide a wide range of services including personal savings, business loans, mortgage solutions, and advanced digital banking tools.
