## Strengthening Your Account Security
Your account security is a shared responsibility. ZX Bank provides robust tools to help you protect your financial data.

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
2FA adds an extra layer of security by requiring two forms of identification to access your account.
### How to Enable 2FA
1. Log in to the ZX Bank Mobile App.
2. Go to 'Settings' > 'Security Center'.
3. Select 'Two-Factor Authentication'.
4. Choose your preferred method:
   - **SMS Code**: A one-time code sent to your registered mobile number.
   - **Authenticator App**: Use apps like Google Authenticator or Authy for time-based codes.
   - **ZX Secure Token**: A push notification sent to your trusted mobile device.

## Strong Password Guidelines
- Use a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
- Avoid using easily guessable information like birthdays or common words.
- Use a unique password for each of your online accounts.

## Security Alerts
Sign up for security alerts to be notified about:
- Password changes
- Logins from new devices
- Large withdrawals or transfers
- Changes to your contact information
