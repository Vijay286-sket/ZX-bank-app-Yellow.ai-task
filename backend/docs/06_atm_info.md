## Finding an ATM
ZX Bank has a vast network of over 50,000 fee-free ATMs across the country through our partnership with the AllPoint network.

## Using ZX Bank ATMs
Withdrawals, deposits, and balance inquiries at any ZX Bank branded ATM are always free of charge.

## Fees and Limits
### Out-of-Network Fees
- **Domestic**: A fee of $2.50 applies for each withdrawal at a non-ZX Bank or non-partner ATM.
- **International**: A fee of $5.00 applies for international withdrawals, plus a 3% foreign transaction fee.

### Withdrawal Limits
The standard daily ATM withdrawal limit is $1,000. This can be temporarily increased upon request through our customer support or via the app.

## Safety Tips
- Always cover your PIN when entering it.
- Be aware of your surroundings, especially at night.
- If you notice any suspicious devices on an ATM, do not use it and report it immediately.
