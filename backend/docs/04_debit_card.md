## Managing Your ZX Debit Card
Your ZX Bank Debit Card is the key to accessing your funds worldwide. We offer comprehensive tools to manage your card via the mobile app and online banking.

## Activation and PIN
New cards must be activated before use. You can activate your card by:
- Logging into the ZX Bank Mobile App and selecting 'Activate Card'.
- Calling our automated activation line at 1-800-ZX-CARD.
- Using any ZX Bank ATM and entering your initial PIN sent via mail.

## Security Features
### Lock and Unlock
If you misplace your card, use the 'Lock Card' feature in the app to prevent unauthorized transactions. You can unlock it instantly once found.

### Travel Notices
Planning a trip? Set a travel notice in the app to ensure your card works seamlessly in foreign countries and to prevent false fraud flags.

## Limits and Controls
You can set daily spending limits and withdrawal limits directly from the app to help manage your budget and enhance security.
