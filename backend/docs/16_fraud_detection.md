## Protecting Your Finances
ZX Bank uses advanced AI-driven monitoring systems to detect suspicious activity 24/7. Your security is our top priority.

## How We Detect Fraud
- **Unusual Spending Patterns**: Sudden large purchases or transactions in unusual locations.
- **Multiple Failed Logins**: Repeated unsuccessful attempts to access your account.
- **Phishing Detection**: Identifying and blocking malicious links and fraudulent communication attempts.

## What to Do If You Suspect Fraud
If you notice unauthorized transactions or believe your account security has been compromised:
1. **Lock Your Card**: Immediately use the 'Lock Card' feature in the ZX Mobile App.
2. **Report the Activity**: Call our dedicated Fraud Hotline at 1-888-ZX-FRAUD (available 24/7).
3. **Change Your Password**: Update your online banking password and 2FA settings immediately.

## Our Fraud Guarantee
ZX Bank offers $0 Liability for unauthorized transactions reported within 60 days of the statement date. We will investigate and credit your account for valid fraud claims.

## Proactive Security Tips
- Never share your PIN or 2FA codes with anyone, including bank employees.
- Monitor your account activity regularly via the mobile app.
- Enable real-time transaction alerts.
