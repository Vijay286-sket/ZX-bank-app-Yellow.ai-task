## Mobile App Overview
The ZX Bank Mobile App is your all-in-one financial companion, designed for speed, security, and ease of use. Available on both iOS and Android platforms.

## Core Features
### Real-time Account Monitoring
View your balances, transaction history, and pending charges instantly from the dashboard.

### Instant Fund Transfers
Transfer money between your ZX Bank accounts or to other banks locally and internationally with just a few taps.

### Bill Payments
Schedule and pay utility bills, credit card statements, and other service providers directly from the app.

## Security Features
### Biometric Authentication
Use FaceID or Fingerprint scanning for secure and fast login.

### Card Controls
Lock or unlock your debit and credit cards instantly if they are misplaced.

### Push Notifications
Receive real-time alerts for every transaction, ensuring you are always informed of your account activity.
