## Grow Your Savings
At ZX Bank, we offer competitive interest rates to help your money work harder for you. Our rates are reviewed monthly to ensure they remain industry-leading.

## Our Savings Products
### Basic Savings
- **Annual Percentage Yield (APY)**: 0.50%
- **Minimum Balance to Earn APY**: $1
- **Monthly Fee**: None if balance stays above $100

### High-Yield Savings
- **APY**: 4.25%
- **Minimum Balance to Earn APY**: $1,000
- **Monthly Fee**: None if balance stays above $2,500

### Premium Money Market
- **APY**: up to 4.50% (Tiered based on balance)
- **Minimum Balance to Earn APY**: $25,000
- **Monthly Fee**: None if balance stays above $10,000

## How Interest is Calculated
Interest is compounded daily and credited to your account on the last business day of each month.

## Rate Fluctuations
Interest rates are subject to change based on market conditions and Federal Reserve adjustments. Any significant changes will be communicated via your monthly statement.
