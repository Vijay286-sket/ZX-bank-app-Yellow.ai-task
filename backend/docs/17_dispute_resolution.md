## Disputing a Transaction
If you see a charge on your statement that you don't recognize or disagree with (e.g., incorrect amount, duplicate charge, or services not rendered), you have the right to dispute it.

## How to Initiate a Dispute
1. **Gather Documentation**: Collect receipts, invoices, or any correspondence with the merchant.
2. **Contact the Merchant First**: Most issues can be resolved faster by dealing directly with the merchant.
3. **Submit a Dispute to ZX Bank**: If the merchant cannot resolve the issue, you can file a formal dispute via:
   - **Mobile App**: Select the transaction and tap 'Dispute Transaction'.
   - **Online Banking**: Go to 'Account Services' and select 'Dispute Center'.
   - **Phone**: Call our Dispute Resolution team at 1-800-ZX-DISPUTE.

## Timeline for Resolution
- **Initial Review**: We will acknowledge your dispute within 2 business days.
- **Investigation**: We typically resolve disputes within 10 to 45 days.
- **Provisional Credit**: In many cases, we will provide a provisional credit to your account while the investigation is ongoing.

## Possible Outcomes
- **Resolved in Your Favor**: The credit becomes permanent.
- **Resolved in Favor of the Merchant**: The provisional credit is reversed, and the original charge remains.
